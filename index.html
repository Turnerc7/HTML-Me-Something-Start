My Family Trip to Gulf Shores Alabama

My family and I went to Gulf Shores Alabama for a week. We stayed in a beach house right on the beach. With amazing views of the Gulf. The weather was perfect and the water was warm. We spent most of our time on the beach. It was a great trip!

Places to eat

There are a lot of great places to eat in Gulf Shores. Here are a few of our favorites:


  • Picnic Beach - This restaurant is like eating on the beach indoors, the decor is picnic tables and artificial turf for the floor.
  • The Hangout - This place is great, we went to a Pirates and Princesses breakfast. It is a breakfast interactive theater which was great for kids. They have live music everynight and a fun beachy vibe.
  • Rocco's Pizza - This has to be some of the best pizza we had in Gulf Shores!
  • Sunliner Diner - This diner had an amazing atmosphere, from newspaper style menues to a booth that was an actual classic car made into a table

    • Things to do

    There are plenty of things to do in Gulf Shores. Here are a few that we did during our trip:

    • Beach - The beach is amazing in Gulf Shores. We spent most of our time there. The water was warm. We even saw dolphins swimming by!
    • Waterville USA - This is a great water park for kids. They have a lazy river, wave pool, and lots of water slides. It was a lot of fun!
    • Gulf State Park - This is a great place to go for a hike or bike ride.
    • The Wharf - This is a great place to go for shopping and dining. They have lots of stores and restaurants to choose from.

    Where to stay

    There are lots of great places to stay in Gulf Shores.

    • Condo - We stayed in a condo right on the beach. Our balcony overlooked the gulf.
    • Beach Houses - There are lots of beach houses to choose from in Gulf Shores. They are a great option if you want to be close to the beach and have all the amenities of home.
    • Hotel - There are lots of hotels to choose from in Gulf Shores. They are a great option if you want to be close to the beach and want to stay on a good budget.

    Conclusion

    Gulf Shores is a great place to visit with your family. There are lots of things to do and see. We had a great time and can't wait to go back!
